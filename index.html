CCAC Plugin Example

This simple example shows how you can send and receive messages with the CCAC Plugin.

When you enter a search term here and click 'Send to Salesforce', the message is sent to the surrounding Visualforce page, which does a screen pop and searches for whatever you typed. This simulates a search term coming in from a Contact Center application.

When you click a phone number on a Contact inside Salesforce, the number will be sent to this page and displayed below. If using a Contact Center, you would send the phone number to it and call it instead of simply displaying it here.
